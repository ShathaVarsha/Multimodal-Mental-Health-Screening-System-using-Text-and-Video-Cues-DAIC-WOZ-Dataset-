What This Assessment Does

  • Comprehensive Screening: Evidence-based questionnaires (PHQ-9 for depression, PCL-5 for PTSD)
  • Video Analysis: Analyzes facial expressions and micro-expressions during the assessment
  • Natural Language: Answer questions in your own words - our AI understands conversational responses
  • Detailed Results: Severity scoring, feature importance analysis, and clinical recommendations
  • Privacy First: All recordings are processed locally and securely
